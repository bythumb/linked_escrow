What We Do

The Neutral Third Party Your Transaction Needs

Escrow is the backbone of every California real estate transaction. As an independent, DFPI-licensed escrow company, Linked Escrow holds funds and documents impartially — protecting buyers, sellers, and lenders equally until all conditions are met.

Neutral & Independent

As a DFPI-licensed independent escrow company — not a title company, attorney, or broker — we are exclusively focused on serving as a neutral fiduciary for your transaction.

Secure Fund Handling

Your earnest money deposit and all transaction funds are held in secure, segregated trust accounts — fully compliant with California Financial Code §17000–17606.

Precise Document Handling

We prepare, track, and manage all escrow instructions, grant deeds, lender docs, and closing statements — ensuring nothing falls through the cracks.

The Process

From Open to Close, We Handle It All

Every step of your transaction is managed by a dedicated escrow officer — so nothing falls through the cracks from the day escrow opens to the day funds are disbursed.

Open an Escrow
1
Open Escrow
Submit your purchase contract or refinance docs. We issue escrow instructions within one business day.
2
Deposit Funds
Earnest money and closing funds are deposited into our secure, DFPI-supervised trust accounts.
3
Clear Conditions
We coordinate loan docs, title clearance, and all contingency conditions with all parties.
4
Sign & Record
Documents are signed, the grant deed is recorded with the county, and title transfers.
5
Close & Disburse
Funds are disbursed to all parties — seller proceeds, agent commissions, payoffs, and fees — within 24 hours of closing.

DFPI Licensed

California's Highest Standard of Escrow Regulation

Linked Escrow is a licensed independent escrow company regulated by the California Department of Financial Protection and Innovation (DFPI) under the California Escrow Law (Financial Code §17000–17606). Independent escrow companies provide the highest level of consumer protection and are supervised exclusively for the purpose of acting as a neutral escrow agent.
State Licensed

Our license is issued and maintained under the California Escrow Law, enforced by DFPI — the gold standard for consumer protection in California escrow.

View License Details →
Annual Audits

As required by the Escrow Law, we undergo an independent annual CPA audit and submit reports to the DFPI Commissioner — ensuring rigorous financial oversight.

Bonded & Insured

We maintain a surety bond as required by Financial Code §17312 and carry all required fidelity bonds — protecting your funds against any misappropriation.
