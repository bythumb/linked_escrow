Specialized Services

More Ways We Can Help

FSBO Transactions

Selling or buying without an agent? We provide neutral, professional escrow services and will guide you through what's needed to close safely — including document checklists, title coordination, and fund disbursement.

Vacant Land

Raw land, lot purchases, agricultural parcels, and development sites. We coordinate with surveyors, environmental consultants, and title officers for clean closings on complex land deals.

Short Sales & REO

Bank-approved short sales and real estate owned (REO) properties require precise timeline management and lender coordination. Our team is experienced with distressed asset closings that require extra care.

New Construction

Builder transactions including builder sales, custom home escrow, and progress draw coordination for construction loans and phased developments.

Bulk & Portfolio Sales

Multiple properties closing simultaneously? We can manage bulk acquisition and portfolio sale escrows, assigning dedicated officers to coordinate parallel closings on your timeline.

1031 Exchange Escrow

We work with qualified intermediaries on Section 1031 tax-deferred exchanges — coordinating both the relinquished property and replacement property escrow for seamless investor closings.
