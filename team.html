Beth
Escrow Officer & Founder
40+ Years in Real Estate Industry 24+ Years Escrow Specialization
Beth@linkedescrow.com

A home is more than just a place — it is where life unfolds, memories are made, and new chapters begin. That is why Beth considers it a true privilege to be part of each client's journey.

With more than 40 years in the real estate industry and 24 years of specialized escrow experience, Beth brings seasoned expertise, steady guidance, and a deep understanding of the transaction process to every file. Her background includes residential sales, refinances, REO properties, short sales, and mobile home transfers through Housing and Community Development and the DMV, giving her a uniquely well-rounded perspective.

Whether working with a first-time homebuyer or a seasoned real estate professional, Beth is committed to making the escrow process smooth, strategic, and stress-free. Her goal is to bring clarity, confidence, and care to every step, helping ensure each closing feels like a successful move forward.
